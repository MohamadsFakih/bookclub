Book Club
-Every person picks 1 book
-We read that book at a certain pace of pages.
-Google sheets that has statistics about each book including rating and page length.
-Group chat to talk about the book.
-A day to rate and discuss the book.

Use Cases
-Be able to create/join a group with people.
-To give a rating of the book once done.
-To review the book once done.
-To pick a book for the bookclub.
-To access statistics about previous books the book club read. Including most liked.
-Group chat feature.
-Show due date to finish the book.
-Create user profile
-Login/Signup
